SightCare Ingredients:

Astaxanthin: An antioxidant with potential benefits for eye health, including reducing inflammation and protecting against age-related macular degeneration (AMD).
Quercetin: An antioxidant with anti-inflammatory properties that may support eye health, but research is limited.
N-Acetyl-L-Cysteine (NAC): An antioxidant that may help protect eye cells from oxidative damage.
Zeaxanthin and Lutein: Carotenoids found in the macula, a part of the eye responsible for central vision. These may protect against AMD and blue light damage.
L-Lysine: An amino acid with limited evidence for its role in eye health.
Eyebright: An herb traditionally used for eye health, but scientific evidence for its effectiveness is limited.
Bilberry Extract: Contains antioxidants and may improve night vision, with some preliminary research suggesting potential benefits for eye health.
Other Ingredients: Ascorbic acid, copper gluconate, and zinc oxide, all of which have various roles in overall health but limited specific evidence for eye health benefits.

Antioxidant Support: Several ingredients like bilberry extract and NAC offer antioxidant properties, potentially benefiting eye health.
